# OlchiLab Web Design Brief

## Position

`www.olchilab.com` is the company and business hub for OlchiLab.

OlchiClass is a sub-brand for MBL classes and AI lectures, not the whole company.

## Core Message

필요한 것을 먼저 알아차리고, 실행 가능한 형태로 정리합니다.

## 10-Second Message

올치랩은 학교·기관과 협업해 교육 운영과 실무를 정리하는 소규모 사업체이며, 교육은 하위 브랜드 올치클래스에서 MBL 수업과 AI 기초특강으로 운영합니다.

## Brand Line

옳게 보고, 꼼꼼하게 맞춘다.

## Primary Reference

Airtable editorial workflow landing.

Use:

- white canvas
- dark ink CTA
- calm hero
- one strong signature band
- restrained cards with 10-12px radius
- table/list rhythm instead of decorative card repetition

Do not copy Airtable colors, logo, customer logos, pricing pill system, or marketing copy.

## Home vs OlchiClass Boundary

The OlchiLab home should introduce OlchiClass only as a sub-brand.

Keep on the OlchiLab home:

- what OlchiLab is
- what OlchiLab organizes
- the relationship between OlchiLab and OlchiClass
- who the work is for
- how OlchiLab works
- contact paths

Move to an OlchiClass page:

- detailed MBL class descriptions
- detailed AI lecture descriptions
- curriculum
- class materials
- student/teacher workflows
- detailed school or institution program information

## Navigation Rule

Do not use role-based top navigation for the first version.

Avoid:

- 학교
- 기관
- 교사
- 학부모
- 학생

Use topic/task navigation instead:

- 올치랩 소개
- 하는 일
- 올치클래스
- 협업 방식
- 문의

Explain audiences inside sections instead of forcing visitors to self-select a role first.

## First-Version Scope

Include:

- OlchiLab one-line definition
- OlchiLab / OlchiClass relationship
- 3 core work areas
- OlchiClass summary and 2 program summaries
- collaboration range
- operator/contact/email
- contact section
- privacy policy
- honest current-state notes

Defer:

- testimonials
- pricing table
- full curriculum
- PDF proposal download
- institution-specific portfolio
- blog/news section
- large FAQ page
- quote calculator
- registration system
- large photo/video gallery

## Guardrails

- Do not make OlchiLab look like only an education brand.
- Do not make unfinished projects look like launched services.
- Do not overuse Sensor Lime.
- Keep Deep Navy and Warm Paper as the main visual rhythm.
- Do not make the first screen effectively an OlchiClass lecture page.
- Do not place detailed MBL or AI lecture content on the OlchiLab home.
- Do not use vague top-nav labels like "Solutions", "Insights", or "Programs" for the first version.
- Do not claim testimonials, partners, pricing, or outcomes that are not ready.
- Do not collect student/minor personal information directly in the first-version contact flow.
