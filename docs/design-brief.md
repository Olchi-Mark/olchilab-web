# OlchiLab Web Design Brief

## Position

`www.olchilab.com` is the company and business hub for OlchiLab.

OlchiClass is a sub-brand for MBL classes and AI lectures, not the whole company.

## Core Message

필요한 것을 먼저 알아차리고, 실행 가능한 형태로 정리합니다.

## Brand Line

옳게 보고, 꼼꼼하게 맞춘다.

## Color Tokens

| Role | Name | HEX |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Primary | Deep Navy | `#1B3556` |
| Secondary | Active Teal | `#17777A` |
| Accent | Sensor Lime | `#A8CF45` |
| Background | Warm Paper | `#F5F7F2` |
| Text | Graphite | `#24323D` |
| Optional | Safety Orange | `#E98A2B` |

## Primary Reference

Airtable editorial workflow landing.

Use:

- white canvas
- dark ink CTA
- calm hero
- one strong signature band
- restrained cards with 10-12px radius
- table/list rhythm instead of decorative card repetition

Do not copy Airtable colors, logo, customer logos, pricing pill system, or marketing copy.

## Home Sections

1. Hero
2. What We Organize
3. OlchiClass
4. Projects Under OlchiLab
5. How We Work
6. Contact

## Guardrails

- Do not make OlchiLab look like only an education brand.
- Do not make unfinished projects look like launched services.
- Do not overuse Sensor Lime.
- Keep Deep Navy and Warm Paper as the main visual rhythm.
